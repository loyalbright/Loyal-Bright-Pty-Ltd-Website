About Loyal Bright Accountants

Expertise, Integrity, and Personalized Financial Guidance in Melbourne.

Our Story

Established in 2019 and led by Principal Tony Geng, Loyal Bright Accountants is a boutique CPA Public Practice based in Balwyn, Victoria. Our firm was founded on a simple premise: small business owners, investors, and individuals deserve access to top-tier financial expertise without the overwhelming corporate jargon.

Our CPAs are alumni of Australia’s top-tier universities, holding advanced degrees including the Master of Professional Accounting and Master of Commerce from the University of Melbourne and Deakin University. We combine this rigorous academic background with years of practical, on-the-ground experience navigating the complexities of the Australian taxation system.

Our Objective

Our objective is to bridge the gap between your financial aspirations and the technical knowledge required to achieve them. We don't just process tax returns; we aim to deliver proactive advice and constructive solutions that you can rely on for long-term wealth creation and business growth.

Whether you are navigating Division 7A compliance, structuring a new Pty Ltd company, or optimizing your property investment portfolio, we are committed to providing clarity and peace of mind.
