Our Professional Services

Comprehensive tax, accounting, and advisory services designed to optimize your financial position and ensure total compliance.

At Loyal Bright Accountants, we serve a diverse portfolio of clients, from individual taxpayers to complex corporate groups and property developers. Explore our core service areas below.

Business Advisory & Tax

We provide proactive tax planning and structural advice to help your business grow while remaining fully compliant with ATO regulations.

  • Pty Ltd, Trust, and Partnership setup
  • Division 7A loan compliance
  • Annual company tax returns
  • Business diagnostics & cashflow forecasting

Property Investment Tax

Real estate taxation is highly complex. We guide investors and developers through the nuances of Australian property tax law to optimize returns.

  • Capital Gains Tax (CGT) planning
  • Negative gearing optimization
  • Main Residence Exemption analysis
  • Land tax & Stamp duty advice

Individual Tax Returns

We ensure you receive your maximum entitled refund by identifying all legitimate deductions applicable to your specific industry and circumstances.

  • Complex individual tax returns
  • Contractor & Sole Trader taxation
  • Share trading & crypto tax reporting
  • Expat & foreign income advice

SMSF Tax & Compliance

Take control of your retirement. We manage the heavy lifting of Self-Managed Super Fund compliance so you can focus on your investment strategy.

  • SMSF financial statement preparation
  • Annual SMSF tax returns
  • Audit preparation & coordination
  • Pension phase transition advice

Bookkeeping & Payroll

Reclaim your time. We seamlessly integrate with your cloud accounting software to maintain accurate, real-time financial records.

  • Xero, MYOB, and QuickBooks management
  • Single Touch Payroll (STP Phase 2)
  • Superannuation Guarantee (SG) processing
  • BAS & IAS preparation and lodgement

Corporate Secretarial

Avoid late fees and penalties. We act as your registered agent to ensure your corporate governance remains up to date.

  • ASIC Registered Agent services
  • Annual company statement reviews
  • Director & Shareholder changes
  • Registered office facility
